Mike Etten is a musician and composer living in New York City. He is an original member of the NYC-based experimental band PC Worship, and currently plays guitar in the Dougie Poole band. Etten played in a variety of other projects throughout the 2010s, including Ashcan Orchestra, Cavex, and LuxLuxLuster, and was one of the founders of the Bushwick venue and house collective Le Wallet. Having done extensive touring throughout North America and Europe with PC Worship, sharing stages with such acts as the Sun Ra Arkestra, Arto Lindsay, Panda Bear, Mac DeMarco, and Parquet Courts, Etten has also entered into the world of film music, composing for an HBO comedy special in 2019 and a short film entitled "In the Flesh" that screened at Sundance in 2022. His debut solo LP released on October 1, 2021 on Dull Tools.
